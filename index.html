⚙️ How the hCASH LP Moat Works
The hCASH LP Moat is a special liquidity provision moat. Unlike regular moats where you stake the token directly, here you:
STEP 1 — PROVIDE LP
Pair equal-value AVAX + hCASH on Pharaoh Exchange. You receive Volatile LP tokens representing your share of the pool. This earns you trading fees 24/7.
STEP 2 — DEPOSIT LP
Stake or Lock your LP tokens in the hCASH Moat. Stake for 1× points with flexible exit. Lock for 90–730 days to earn 2.73× to 5.00× multipliers.
